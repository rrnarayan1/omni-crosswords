Welcome to OmniCrosswords!

This is an iOS app that allows you to complete crosswords. It fetches the crosswords from major outlets, like the LA Times, USA Today, Wall Street Journal, and more.

  • Discover

What is this app?

OmniCrosswords is an app that I made because there wasn't an app on iOS that fetches crosswords from the major newspapers and allows users to complete them.

Currently, I support these outlets:

  • LA Times
  • USA Today
  • The Atlantic
  • Newsday
  • The New Yorker
  • The Wall Street Journal

I give a huge credit to AlphaCross , which inspired me to develop this app.
