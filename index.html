What is this app?

Omni Crosswords is an iOS app that fetches crosswords from major outlets and allows users to complete them.

Currently, I support these outlets:

  • LA Times
  • USA Today
  • The Atlantic
  • Newsday
  • The New Yorker
  • The Wall Street Journal
  • New York Times Syndicated

AlphaCross inspired me to develop this iOS application.

Thanks to xword-dl as well
